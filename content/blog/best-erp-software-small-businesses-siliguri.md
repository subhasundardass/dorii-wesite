## 2. Problems Local Businesses Face Every Day

Before we talk about solutions, let us understand the real problems that Siliguri business owners deal with on a daily basis. If you run a shop, a warehouse, a transport company, or a wholesale business here, you will recognise at least one of these.

### 📒 Manual Accounting Errors

Writing entries by hand or in basic Tally leads to calculation mistakes, missing transactions, and wrong balance sheets at month-end.

### 📦 Stock Mismatch Problems

The stock in your system says 200 pieces but the godown shows 170. Nobody knows where 30 items went. This happens constantly without proper inventory tracking.

### 🧾 GST Filing Stress

Collecting invoices from staff, matching purchase data, calculating HSN codes manually every month is a nightmare — especially when GSTR deadlines are near.

### 🏪 Managing Multiple Branches

If you have a shop in Siliguri and another in Jalpaiguri, tracking combined sales, stock transfers, and total profit manually is nearly impossible.

### 👥 Staff Salary, Attendance & Advances

Keeping track of employees’ attendance, calculating salaries, and managing advances manually is time-consuming and prone to errors. Mistakes in payroll or missing leave records can lead to dissatisfaction and conflicts, especially when you have multiple staff across different branches.

> 💡 **Real example from Siliguri:** A wholesale grocery trader in Champasari Market was losing approximately ₹15,000–20,000 per month due to stock discrepancies and manual billing errors. After switching to a custom ERP, they tracked every transaction and brought losses down to near zero within 3 months.

## 3. Key Features an ERP Should Have

Not every ERP is the same. If you are a business owner in Siliguri looking for ERP software, here are the features that actually matter for local businesses — not fancy things you will never use.

### Inventory Management

- ✅ Real-time stock tracking across all locations and godowns
- ✅ Low stock alerts so you never run out of fast-moving items
- ✅ Batch and expiry tracking for perishables, medicines, or FMCG products
- ✅ Barcode scanning support for fast billing at the counter

### GST Billing and Compliance

- ✅ Auto-generation of GST-compliant invoices with correct HSN codes
- ✅ GSTR-1, GSTR-3B, and e-Invoice reports ready to upload directly
- ✅ Auto-calculation of CGST, SGST, and IGST based on buyer location
- ✅ E-way bill generation for goods transport — critical for Siliguri transport companies

### Warehouse Management

- ✅ Multi-location warehouse tracking (Main godown, secondary store, retail shelf)
- ✅ Goods receipt and dispatch management with documentation
- ✅ Damage and return tracking with proper accounting entries

### Multi-Branch Support

- ✅ Centralised dashboard showing all branch sales, stock, and cash in one screen
- ✅ Stock transfer management between branches with approval flow
- ✅ Branch-wise profit and loss reporting
- ✅ Role-based access — branch manager can only see their branch data

### Other Must-Have Features

- ✅ Purchase order and vendor management with payment tracking
- ✅ Customer ledger — how much each customer owes you (udhaar management)
- ✅ Staff attendance and basic payroll for small teams
- ✅ Mobile-friendly dashboard so you can check reports from your phone

## 6. How to Choose an ERP Development Company in Siliguri

Choosing the right software development partner is just as important as choosing the right features. Here is a simple checklist to help you pick the right ERP company in Siliguri.

→ **Local presence matters** — Choose a company based in Siliguri or North Bengal. They understand local business practices, GST regulations for West Bengal, and can visit your office when needed — not just call on phone.

→ **Ask to see past work** — A good ERP company will show you live examples of software they have built for other businesses. Ask for client references in similar industries.

→ **Understand how they gather requirements** — The best developers spend time at your office understanding how you work before writing a single line of code. If a company gives you a quote without visiting, be careful.

→ **Check post-delivery support** — ERP is not a one-time thing. Your business will change, GST rules will change, your team will grow. Make sure the company offers long-term support and annual maintenance.

→ **Ask about GST compliance updates** — India's GST rules change every year. Your ERP provider should update the software whenever new rules come in — without charging you each time.

→ **Avoid very cheap quotes** — If someone quotes you ₹5,000 for a full ERP system, walk away. Real custom ERP takes weeks of work. Suspiciously cheap quotes usually mean a half-finished product or they disappear after payment.

→ **Insist on a demo first** — Always ask for a working demo or prototype before signing any contract. A good company will happily show you how the system will work.

The right ERP company is not the cheapest or the most expensive — it is the one that understands your business, communicates clearly, delivers on time, and stands behind their product for years.
