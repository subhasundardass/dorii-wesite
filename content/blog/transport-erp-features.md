## 1. Booking & Order Management

The foundation of any transport business is booking management.

Your Transport ERP must support:

- Consignment / GR / LR creation  
- Multi-point pickup & delivery  
- Customer-wise rate management  
- Automatic freight calculation  
- Load planning and allocation  

With proper booking management, transporters can avoid manual errors, reduce paperwork, and track every shipment from origin to destination.

---

## 2. Hub & Inventory Management

For businesses operating across multiple cities, hub-based tracking is critical.

A good Transport ERP should provide:

- Hub-wise stock visibility  
- Incoming & outgoing shipment tracking  
- Hub-to-hub transfer management  
- Real-time inventory status  
- Shortage & damage recording  

This ensures complete control over goods movement and reduces misplacement or theft risks.

---

## 3. Manifest & Trip Management

Manifest and trip management is the heart of transport operations.

Essential features include:

- Vehicle assignment  
- Driver allocation  
- Route planning  
- Manifest generation  
- Load optimization  
- Trip expense tracking  

A well-designed system ensures maximum vehicle utilization and better operational control.

---

## 4. Route Optimization

Fuel is one of the biggest expenses for Indian transporters.

Transport ERP software should include:

- Route optimization  
- Distance calculation  
- Toll estimation  
- Alternative route suggestions  
- Traffic-based adjustments  

Optimized routes reduce fuel consumption, delivery delays, and vehicle wear & tear.

---

## 5. Real-Time Tracking & Visibility

Customers today demand transparency.

Modern Transport ERP systems must integrate:

- GPS tracking  
- Live vehicle location  
- Shipment tracking links  
- Delivery status updates  
- Automated SMS / WhatsApp notifications  

Real-time visibility increases customer trust and reduces support calls.

---

## 6. Billing & Financial Management

Accurate billing is crucial for profitability.

Your ERP should support:

- Auto invoice generation  
- Customer-wise credit limits  
- GST-compliant invoices  
- Multi-rate billing  
- Cash & credit tracking  
- Outstanding management  

Integrated accounting prevents revenue leakage and improves cash flow management.

---

## 7. Driver & Vehicle Management

Managing fleet and drivers efficiently ensures smooth operations.

Key features:

- Vehicle maintenance schedules  
- Insurance & permit tracking  
- Driver document management  
- Fuel consumption reports  
- Performance tracking  

This helps avoid compliance issues and unexpected breakdowns.

---

## 8. Role-Based Access Control

Not everyone in your company needs access to everything.

A professional Transport ERP must offer:

- User roles (Admin, Operations, Accounts, Hub Manager)  
- Permission-based access  
- Activity logs  
- Audit trails  

This ensures security and accountability within the organization.

---

## 9. Reports & Business Analytics

Data-driven decisions improve profitability.

Your ERP should generate:

- Profit per trip reports  
- Fuel expense analysis  
- Hub performance reports  
- Customer revenue analysis  
- Vehicle utilization reports  

Clear reporting helps transporters identify loss-making routes and optimize operations.

---

## 10. Scalability & Customization

Indian transport businesses grow quickly.

Your Transport ERP should:

- Support multiple branches  
- Allow workflow customization  
- Integrate with third-party tools  
- Be cloud-enabled  
- Offer mobile access  

Scalability ensures your software grows with your business.

A well-built Transport ERP system addresses these challenges by bringing automation, transparency, and operational efficiency.
